Unit Circle Quiz

Timed tests are the worst, especially ones dealing with the unit circle. However, if you use this app can make it a lot easier. This app quizzes you on where angles lie on the unit circle, in radians and degrees. This helps significantly because it helps you imagine angles in your head and thus, find the sin, cos and tan values by just imagining the y coordinate, x coordinate and slope of the radius.
