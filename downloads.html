Unit Circle Quiz

Timed tests are the worst, especially ones dealing with the unit circle. However, if you use this app can make it a lot easier. This app quizzes one on how angles, in radians and degrees. Given an angle, one must find where on the circle the radius lies. This helps significantly because it helps you imagine angles in your head and thus, find the sin, cos and tan values.
